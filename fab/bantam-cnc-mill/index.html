    Fixturing

    • Double Sided Tape - Works great with flat, thin material like circuit boards, thin metal, or plastic pieces.
    • Hot Glue - A bead of hot glue around the edges of larger or rougher materials — like wood, plastic, and machining wax — works great.
    • T-Slots - These little slots in the bed can be used to hold material to the bed. For Advanced use.
